product subcategories

  • Steel Tubes for Precision Applications

    Our knowledge and experience in producing precision tubes allows us to be at the forefront of new product development and to offer a huge variety of formats.

    When dimensional accuracy and surface finish are the priority, circular, square or rectangular tube sections for general use can be used.

    Applications

    • Furniture
    • Metal structures
    • Bicycles
    • Automotive industry
    • Tool development

    Steel is available in the following formats: Cold Rolled Steel, Hot Rolled Pickled and Oiled Steel and Galvanized Steels

    Standard

    • EN 10305-5

  • Cold Formed Welded Structural Hollow Sections

    The use of welded structural hollow sections in metallic or composite structures is of great added value to the construction industry.

    Due to their relatively low weight and ease of maintenance, they are widely used in large projects. Production of these sections is carried under several resistance categories according to the EC3 Standard.

    Applications

    • Automotive Industry, Furniture Industry
    • Tool Development
    • Metal Construction

    Standard

    • EN 10219-1/2

  • Steel Tubes Suitable For Welding And Threading

    With a proven reliability lasting several years, products conforming to the EN 10255 standard are suitable for a wide range of end uses.

    All our products are subject to Hydrostatic Testing in accordance with standard pressures and Non-destructive Testing (Eddy Current testing ECT) during the production process. This rigorous control process guarantees meticulous structural design and complete traceability.

    Applications

    • Industrial or domestic fluid-conducting networks
    • Fire safety system, extinguishers

    Standards

    • EN10255
    • EN10240 A1 e A2

  • Special Sections

    Our special sections are tubes that are welded lengthways and cold formed.

    They can be applied to various fields, given the variety of geometric shapes in which they are produced.

    Applications

    • Handrails
    • Architecture
    • Construction Industry

    Types available: Handrails, Miscellaneous W.O., Various Section Shapes (Oval, Elliptical, flat sided oval, semi-flat sided oval, semi-trapezoidal section, semi-oblique section), and Decorative Tubes

    Standard

    • EN 10305
